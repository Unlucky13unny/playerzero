FAQs

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

A weekly community competition for players who want to show off their grind. It's not affiliated with any game publisher — just a competition for bragging rights and prizes.

How do I join?

Click the "Enter Now" button, pay the entry fee, and upload a screenshot of your Trainer Code screen.

How is it scored?

Top players are ranked by XP gain, distance walked, Pokémon caught, and social challenges — all manually submitted and verified.

What do I win?

Cash for the top scorers. Bonus prizes for social media challenges. Prize pool scales with participation.

What prevents cheating?

Each entry is verified using image-reading tools and a set of internal checks. If anything looks altered or suspicious, it’s disqualified — no exceptions.
