Frequently Asked Questions

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

A weekly competition where players earn points based on in-game progress and complete social media challenges.

How do I join?

Click the "Enter Now" button above, pay the entry fee, and upload a screenshot of your Trainer Code screen.

What do I need to win?

Top scores in XP gain, distance walked, Pokémon caught, PokéStops visited, and participation in bonus challenges.

What are the prizes?

Cash prizes for top overall scorers and daily social challenge winners. Prize tiers increase with more participants.

Is cheating allowed?

Absolutely not. All submissions are verified, and any fake data or altered screenshots will result in disqualification.
