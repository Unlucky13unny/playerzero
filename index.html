FAQs

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

A competition built for the grind. Compete using in-game Pokémon GO stats and social media challenges. It’s not affiliated with Niantic — just bragging rights, cash, and community pride.

How do I join?

Click “Enter Now,” pay the fee, and upload your Trainer Code screen. That code becomes your PlayerZero ID — used to verify your performance throughout the week-long event.

When does the challenge start?

Once the minimum participant tier is reached, we’ll set a launch date — with plenty of advance notice (typically 30+ days) to plan, train, and hype it up. We want this to be huge, not just minimum.

What stats count?

This challenge is all about the grind — tracked through quantifiable stats like XP gain, Pokémon caught, and more. The final categories may vary depending on participation and could include weighted scoring or single-stat formats. Full scoring rules will be posted before the event begins.

Does player level matter?

Nope. Whether you’re a level 12 newbie or level 50 vet, scoring is based on your week’s hustle — not lifetime stats. Everyone starts on equal footing.

What do I win?

Cash for top performers. Bonus prizes for social media challenges. Prize tiers scale with participation — more players = more winners and bigger payouts.

What does the entry fee go toward?

Everything goes back into the challenge — covering costs, prize money, tools, and community support.

What if I miss the start?

You can still play! The challenge runs for 7 days, and your starting stats can be submitted anytime during the event window. Just make sure both start and end stats are uploaded to qualify.

How do I know if I won?

Winners will be contacted via the email you submit at signup. You can also follow updates and leaderboard info on Instagram at @pgPlayerZero.

Can I cheat?

Nope. All entries are reviewed using AI tools and human moderation. Any altered images, suspicious data, or funny business = disqualified with no refund. Keep it fair, or don’t bother.
