FAQs

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

This is a competitive community event for Pokémon GO players. Compete across XP gained, Pokémon caught, distance walked, and PokéStops visited for glory and prizes. Not affiliated with any game publisher.

What's new for this event?

Winners will be crowned across multiple categories. Plus, 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishers will be honored based on a weighted total across all four categories for the ultimate PlayerZero Podium.

Prizes: 75K PokéCoins for 1st, 60K for 2nd, and 45K for 3rd.

Final prize counts may grow depending on total participation.

How do I enter?

Lock in your spot by submitting your Trainer Code, starting stats screenshots, and entry fee via the registration form.

How are winners decided?

Top performers will be determined in each individual discipline and overall combined. Top performers can only win either a category prize or a podium prize — not both.

Is there anything else happening?

Stay sharp. Random bonus challenges may drop on social media for extra prizes during the event!

Where can I follow updates?

Follow us on Instagram @pgPlayerZero and TikTok @pgPlayerZero for daily highlights, bonus drops, and winner shoutouts.
