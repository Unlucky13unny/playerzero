FAQs

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

A community competition for players who want to show off their grind. Not affiliated with any game publisher — just bragging rights and prizes.

How do I join?

Click “Enter Now,” pay the entry fee, and upload your Trainer Code screenshot. That code will be your ID for the event.

When does the challenge start?

Once we hit the minimum threshold, we’ll announce a launch date at least 30 days out — giving you plenty of time to prepare, rally others, and plan your grind.

What stats count?

This challenge scores based on quantifiable grind — XP, catches, distance walked, and more. Final categories may vary and will be announced before launch.

What do I win?

Cash prizes for top scorers in individual and combined stat categories. Bonus prizes for social media submissions. The more players, the more prizes.

What prevents cheating?

We verify all submissions with screenshots, AI readers, and additional checks. Fake or altered images result in instant disqualification. No refunds. No shortcuts.

Will player level be a factor?

Nope. Everyone starts equal. Your grind during the challenge is all that matters.

How do I track updates or get support?

We’ll contact winners by email and post updates on Instagram: @pgPlayerZero. Questions? Use the contact form above.

What if I miss the start?

The challenge lasts 7 days. You can lock in your start stats anytime during that window — so even if you start late, you can still finish strong.

Where does my entry fee go?

100% supports the prize pool and infrastructure for PlayerZero. The more entries, the more rewards for the community.
