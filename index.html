FAQs

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

This is a community competition for Pokémon GO players. It’s not affiliated with any game publisher — just a grind-it-out contest for bragging rights, stats, and prizes.

How do I join?

Submit your Trainer Code, stats, and screenshots through the entry form and pay the entry fee to lock in your spot.

When does the challenge start?

Once we hit the minimum number of players to fund the base prize pool, we'll set a launch date. You’ll be notified well in advance so you have time to prepare and plan your grind.

What stats count?

This challenge is all about the grind — tracked through quantifiable stats like XP gain, Pokémon caught, distance walked, and PokéStops visited. Final categories and scoring methods will be shared before the challenge begins and may shift based on participation.

What do I win?

Cash prizes for top scorers in core stat categories. Bonus rewards for social challenge standouts. The more players who enter, the bigger the prize pool — and the more winners in each tier.

How are winners notified?

We'll contact winners via the email submitted on your entry form. You can also follow us on Instagram @pgPlayerZero to stay in the loop and participate in community shoutouts.

What if I miss the start?

The challenge runs for a full 7 days. You can join late and still submit both your starting and ending stats. Just make sure you complete both submission forms before the deadline!

Will player level be a scoring factor?

No. All scoring metrics are chosen to level the playing field. Whether you’re a new or veteran trainer, the categories are designed to reward hustle — not legacy advantage.

What prevents cheating?

We require screenshots before and after the challenge. These are scanned using image verification tools and reviewed manually. We also have unique Buddy Pokémon name verification to confirm identity across submissions. Any suspicious or manipulated images will result in disqualification — no refunds.
