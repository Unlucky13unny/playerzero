FAQs

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

This is a community competition for Pokémon Go players who want to prove their grind. It’s not affiliated with any game publisher — just a test of effort, hustle, and bragging rights.

How do I join?

Submit your Trainer Code, stats, and screenshots through the entry form and pay the entry fee to lock in your spot.

When does the challenge start?

The challenge will launch once the minimum participation threshold is hit — but we won’t start immediately. You’ll get advance notice (30+ days) so you can prepare, invite others, and plan your grind.

What stats count?

This challenge is all about quantifiable grind — XP gain, Pokémon caught, distance walked, PokéStops visited, and more. The exact scoring system may focus on individual stats or a weighted combo. Final rules will be announced before kickoff.

What do I win?

Cash prizes for top performers, plus bonus awards for things like social challenges. More participants = more prize tiers and more winners per category.

How are winners notified?

Winners will be contacted via the email they provided during signup. We’ll also post results and shoutouts on Instagram @pgPlayerZero.
