FAQs

What is the PlayerZero Challenge?

A community-driven grind-off for Pokémon Go players. Compete using in-game stats and social prompts to win prizes and prove your hustle.

How do I join?

Hit the "Enter Now" button, pay the small entry fee, and upload your Trainer Code to lock in your spot.

When does it start?

Once we reach our minimum player count, we’ll set a start date and give you plenty of notice to prep and invite others.

What stats count?

This challenge is all about the grind — tracked through quantifiable stats like XP gain, Pokémon caught, and more. Final categories will be announced before launch.

Can I still win if I start late?

Yes. The challenge lasts 7 days and you can submit your starting stats any time during that window. Just grind hard and finish strong.

How are winners announced?

We’ll email winners at the address provided during signup. Follow @pgPlayerZero on Instagram for shoutouts and updates.

What prevents cheating?

Submissions require screenshots and confirmation. We use image-reading tools, cross-verification, and human review to catch sketchy stuff. Don’t ruin the fun — keep it real.

Will player level be a category?

Nope. All scored metrics are based on effort, not legacy. New players can grind just as hard as veterans.

What does the entry fee support?

Your entry goes directly to covering challenge operations and building the prize pool. More players = more prizes + more winners.
